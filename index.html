Hello and Welcome!

My name is Nida Ghuman and I am an aspiring web developer based in the Philadelphia region of Pennsylvania. The world of web design can be a confusing place, but fear not! I am here to help and together we can achieve a web design you will love.

Like most people in this field, I came to be here by a curvy route and have worn many hats along the way. My academic background is in Cultural Anthropology, and I have a Postgraduate Masters degree in that area. For many years I worked in academia, teaching Undergraduate courses in Anthropology which also working as a Research Assistant on several projects. It was during this time that I discovered Cyber Anthropology and fell in love with the beauty, diversity and complexity of the virtual space. Fast forward a few years, and here I am, proud to be part of the community of people creating, maintaining and developing this virtual space!
